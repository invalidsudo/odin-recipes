Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped walnuts
  • 3 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 5 tbsp melted butter
  • 3 (8 oz) packages cream cheese
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a medium bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, chopped walnuts, brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and melted butter.
  3. Mix well and press into the bottom of a 9 inch springform pan.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 10 mins.
  5. Remove from oven and allow to cool.
  6. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar together until smooth.
  7. Beat in sour cream and heavy cream.
  8. Blend in the flour and vanilla.
  9. With mixer on low speed, add eggs one at a time.
  10. Pour batter over crust.
  11. Bake in preheated oven for 60-70 mins, or until centre is almost set (filling will be soft).
  12. Refrigerate at least 6 hours or overnight before removing from pan.
